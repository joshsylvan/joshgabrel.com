The Turing Show

Global Gamne Jam 2024 we thought to try our the at the time interesting AI apis.

Disclaimer I am very anti-AI for in general for commercial games. This was a game jam project to explore what we could do with some APIs. Game was not deployed anywhere as I think it is unethical to deploy apps like this.

In the turing show a human will have to blend into a generated TV sitcom. The human will have to improvise lines in a fully voiced AI generated tv show.

One human 'the writer' will have to write lines and blend in to the current scene. The others, 'the viewers' will have to guess which charater in the show is human.
