Fight Twitter

Fight Twitter is a game created by Lucas Rijllart. and myself during a 12 hour hackathon at the University of Sussex.

The intention was to have two users enter their twitter handles and make their profiles fight to the death. To do this we download their profile pictures and put them onto their characters. We wanted to add quotes from their tweets as they fight, but could not complete this within the 12 hours.

Source code is avliable but does not work any more. It uses an old version of the Twitter (RIP) API from 8 years ago.
