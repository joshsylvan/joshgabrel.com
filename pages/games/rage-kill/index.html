Rage Kill

A top down shooter made whilst at University to learn JavaScript. Created without any third party libraries, just plain HTML5 and Canvas.

Made for desktop browsers, but does not scale to mobile screen sizes.

Use WASD keys to move around. Hold the arrow keys to fire your weapon.

Good luck and be careful of how many bullets you have left.
